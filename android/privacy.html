We collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Services depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

  • Account Information: If you choose to register an account to track your progress and sync activity across devices, we may collect your name, email address, password, and profile photo.
  • Social Media Data: If you choose to link your account with social networks (such as Facebook, Google, or Apple Game Center), we may collect your name, profile picture, email address, and user ID associated with that social media account.
  • Customer Support Data: Information you provide when you contact us for help, such as your email address, the content of your request, and screenshots of your problem.

B. Information Automatically Collected

When you access or use the Services, we and our third-party partners automatically collect certain information about your device and your usage:

  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the Services, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers (such as IMEI, IDFA, GAID), MAC address, mobile network information, screen resolution, language, time zone, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the Services, such as gameplay data, levels completed, scores, session duration, timestamps of visits, referring/exit pages, and crash logs.
  • Location Data: We may collect coarse (approximate) location information derived from your IP address (e.g., country, city). We do not collect precise geolocation data (GPS) without your explicit consent.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, beacons, tags, and scripts to analyze trends, administer the website, track users' movements around the website, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as:

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND OPTIONS

Opt-out of Targeted Advertising

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising tracking through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (OFF), or Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising.
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete advertising ID".

GDPR Rights (EEA/UK/Switzerland Users)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data tracking.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Restrict the processing of your data.
  • Object to processing tracking.
  • Data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the CCPA provides you with specific rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the sources, the purposes of collection, and the third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not "sell" personal information in the traditional sense (for money). However, we may "share" identifiers with ad partners for interest-based advertising, which may be considered a sale under CCPA. You have the right to opt-out of this sharing.

To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us via email.
